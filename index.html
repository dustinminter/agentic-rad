The Agentic AI Control Plane: reference architecture, co-branded by Archetype and Snowflake. Data flows left to right. Sources feed the Snowflake Agentic Control Plane, a single account boundary, which contains three layers. First, The Agent (plan, tool-call, respond). Second, Cortex Tools for retrieval and reasoning: Cortex Analyst for structured text-to-SQL, Cortex Search for unstructured retrieval, and Cortex AISQL for LLM functions in SQL. Third, a Governed Data Foundation of RAW, Harmonized, and Analytics zones, governed by Horizon. A governance gate controls what leaves the boundary before results reach the Act and Publish layer.

Objective

Deploy a governed AI agent that reasons over enterprise data inside the Snowflake boundary, acts on external systems only through an audited gateway, and remains within data-security and governance controls end to end.

  • 1Horizon applies tags, masking, RBAC, and lineage to everything the agent can see.
  • 2Sources land via streaming, batch (Openflow), Marketplace, and enterprise apps.
  • 3Boundary: reasoning and data stay inside the Snowflake account.
  • 4Cortex tools retrieve: Analyst (structured), Search (unstructured), AISQL.
  • 5The agent plans, calls tools, orchestrates sub-agents, and responds.
  • 6Natoma enforces identity, policy, and audit on every outbound tool call (acq. announced 27 May 2026).
  • 7Act & publish: governed actions, dashboards, apps, plus tracing and eval.
  • 8Cyera discovers sensitive data and blocks leakage across the estate.
